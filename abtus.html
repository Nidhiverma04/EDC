Who We Are

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) at UIET is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship among students and providing them with the tools, knowledge, and networks they need to succeed in their entrepreneurial journeys. Through workshops, events, and mentorship, we aim to cultivate a culture of innovation and leadership at UIET and beyond.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower aspiring entrepreneurs to transform their ideas into successful ventures. We focus on building an ecosystem that encourages creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning. By providing resources and mentorship, we aim to bridge the gap between education and real-world business opportunities.
